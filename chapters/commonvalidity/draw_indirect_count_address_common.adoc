    been created with ename:VK_BUFFER_USAGE_INDIRECT_BUFFER_BIT usage flag

    pname:countAddressRange.address must: be a multiple of `4`
  * [[VUID-{refpage}-countAddressRange-13116]]
    The count stored in pname:countAddressRange must: be less than or equal
    to slink:VkPhysicalDeviceLimits::pname:maxDrawIndirectCount

    pname:countAddressRange.size must: be greater than or equal to 4
