=== Deprecation by VK_KHR_shader_float16_int8

`<<VK_KHR_shader_float16_int8>>` extension, when
slink:VkPhysicalDeviceShaderFloat16Int8FeaturesKHR::pname:shaderFloat16 is
enabled.
